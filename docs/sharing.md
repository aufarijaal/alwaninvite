# Sharing Your Invitation

Once your invitation is published, you're ready to share it with your guests!

---

## Your Invitation Link

Every invitation has a unique link based on the URL slug you chose when creating it. It looks like this:

```
alwaninvite.my.id/invite/your-slug
```

You can find this link:

- On your invitation card in the **Invitations** list.
- By clicking the **Preview** button — the URL in your browser is your shareable link.

---

## ✨ Personalized Links — Adding a Guest's Name

You can make each invitation feel personal by adding the guest's name to the link. When a guest opens a personalized link, the invitation greets them by name — for example, _"Dear Budi, you are invited to our wedding…"_

To do this, add `?to=GuestName` at the end of the invitation link:

```
alwaninvite.my.id/invite/your-slug?to=Budi
```

| Example                                | What the guest sees                              |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| `.../invite/budi-dan-ani`              | A generic greeting (no name shown).              |
| `.../invite/budi-dan-ani?to=Pak+Hasan` | _"Dear Pak Hasan"_ appears on the invitation. 🎉 |

::: tip
If the guest's name has spaces, replace each space with `+`. For example: `?to=Pak+Hasan`.
:::

## Sending via WhatsApp

WhatsApp is the most popular way to share wedding invitations. Here are a few tips:

- **Use the WhatsApp Message Generator** — Go to [Guests & Wishes](./guests-wishes), add your guests, and use the built-in "Copy WhatsApp Message" button. It automatically includes the guest's name and personalized link.
- **Send as a message, not a link only** — Add a warm personal note before pasting the link.
- **Test the link yourself first** — Open the link once before sending, to make sure everything looks right on mobile.
- **Share the link, not a screenshot** — The live link lets guests RSVP and leave wishes.

---

## Social Media Preview

When you share your invitation link on WhatsApp, Instagram, or Facebook, a **preview card** automatically appears — showing your names and a generated image. This happens automatically and you don't need to do anything extra.

::: tip
Make sure you've filled in the couple's names in your invitation so the preview card looks great when you share it!
:::

---

## ✅ Before You Share — Quick Checklist

Go through this checklist before sending your invitation to guests:

- [ ] The invitation is set to **Published** (not Draft).
- [ ] The couple's names are spelled correctly.
- [ ] All event details (date, time, venue) are correct.
- [ ] You've previewed the invitation on a mobile screen.
- [ ] The invitation link works when you open it in a browser.
- [ ] The URL slug is what you want (you can't easily change it later without breaking old links).
- [ ] Background music plays correctly (if you added music).
- [ ] Gift information is correct.
