## Welcome to Alwan Invite

Alwan Invite is an online platform for creating and sharing **digital wedding invitations**. Instead of printing paper cards, you get a beautiful webpage that you send to guests via WhatsApp, social media, or any messaging app.

Your invitation page can include:

- Event details (Akad Nikah, Reception, and more)
- An interactive map to the venue
- Gift information (bank transfer, e-wallet, gift registry, and more)
- A livestream link for guests who can't attend in person
- Background music that plays automatically
- RSVP and a space for guests to leave wishes

---

## How It Works

**Step 1 — Sign In:** Log in to your account at [alwaninvite.my.id](https://alwaninvite.my.id).

**Step 2 — Create:** Click **"Create New Invitation"** from your Dashboard and fill in your wedding details.

**Step 3 — Share:** Share the invitation link with guests via WhatsApp, Instagram, or any messaging app.

**Step 4 — Track:** View RSVPs, read guest messages, and manage your guest list from the Dashboard.
